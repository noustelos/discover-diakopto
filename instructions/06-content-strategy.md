# 06-Content Strategy & Blog
Focus on SEO-driven storytelling and local authority.

## The Blog Section
- Maintain the name "Blog" for immediate user recognition.
- Topics: History, Mythology, Local Traditions, and Insider Guides.
- Goal: Act as an SEO engine to drive traffic for long-tail keywords (e.g., "Mythology of Vouraikos").

## SEO Goldmine Approach
- Every major activity (Odontotos, Hiking, Cycling, Boat trips) must be treated as a potential standalone SEO landing page.
- Focus on "Contextual Linking": Connect blog stories with the "Plan Your Visit" and "Eat & Drink" sections.

## Brand Voice
- Authentic, welcoming, and informative.
- Avoid "over-tourism" cliches; focus on the unique "Mountain meets Sea" identity.
