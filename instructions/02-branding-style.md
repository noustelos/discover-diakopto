# Visual Identity & Style Guide
The design must feel like a modern Mediterranean travel magazine.

## Typography
- Headings: Poppins
- Body Text: Inter
- Editorial Accents: Playfair Display (Italic for quotes)

## Color Palette
- Primary: #0F4C75 (Deep Aegean Blue)
- Accent: #2EC4B6 (Turquoise)
- Background: #F4F1EC (Sand Beige)
- Text: #334155 (Dark Gray)

## Design Principles
- Minimal design, large photography, generous white space.
- Soft shadows, rounded cards, minimal text.
- Avoid heavy borders and clutter.
