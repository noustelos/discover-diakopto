About Diakopto

Diakopto is a charming coastal town in Achaia, Greece, where the mountains meet the waters of the Corinthian Gulf.

Famous for the historic Odontotos railway and the breathtaking Vouraikos Gorge, it offers a unique blend of nature, history and authentic Greek hospitality.
